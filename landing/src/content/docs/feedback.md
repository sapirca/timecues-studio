## What helps me triage fast

For bugs:

- **A song slug** if it's reproducible on a specific song — that pins
  down the audio you were looking at.
- **Your annotator namespace** (the email you sign in with) — useful
  for inspecting the exact state your annotations are in.
- **A screenshot or a short clip** if it's visual — much faster than
  describing a layout glitch in words.

For features:

- **The workflow you're trying to do** matters more than the specific
  button you wish existed. Often there's a simpler way to land what you
  want than the feature you first sketched.
- **How often this comes up** — once-a-month annoyances and
  every-session frictions need very different cost/benefit math.

## Status of past requests

The [GitHub issue tracker](https://github.com/sapirca/timecues-studio/issues)
is the canonical place to see what's open, what's in flight, and what's
shipped. Closed issues link to the commit that resolved them.
